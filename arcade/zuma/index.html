How To Play

  • Aim: Move mouse or drag touch over the board
  • Shoot: Click / tap / press Space
  • Swap Ball: X, Shift, or right click
  • Screen: Fullscreen button (tries to lock landscape on phones)
  • Goal: Match 3+ marbles and clear the chain before the skull
Tip: break long mixed sections first so later shots have cleaner match angles.
